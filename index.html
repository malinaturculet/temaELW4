What are the Mustatics®?

Mustatics® are our new, innovative earbuds which deliver audio effortlessly and on demand. They use state-of-the-art technology for noise cancellation and they are customizable.

What is original, fresh and unique about them is that they come with a novel sound identifier app, which allows you to find and save over 1 million new songs, podcasts and TV shows which play around you. Afterwards, the items found through the app will be saved on your Bluetooth paired device.

How do Mustatics® work?

This is a question we get often asked. Here is a summary:

  1. Let's say you are in a pub and you like the music you're hearing. Start the app by pushing the special button on the earbuds or on their case.
  2. Our algorithms create a digital fingerprint for the audio and then searches and matches it against our database.
  3. Ta-da! Here is your song! If your device is connected to the Internet and paired with the Mustatics®, then your song will appear in your Mustatics App®; if not, the audio will be saved in your offline playlist and searched after you connect to the Internet.
